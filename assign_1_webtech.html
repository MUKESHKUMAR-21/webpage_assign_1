Manali

Manali is a popular hill station in Himachal Pradesh. It is famous for snow-capped mountains and adventure activities.

Jaipur

Jaipur is known as the Pink City of India. It is famous for forts, palaces, and Rajasthani culture.

Goa

Goa is famous for its beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.

Varanasi

Varanasi is one of the oldest cities in the world. It is a major spiritual center on the banks of River Ganga.

Munnar

Munnar is a hill station in Kerala known for tea plantations and cool climate.
